XOR

Take a look!

This is my first stab at a WebGL-based game framework. No sound or input, but it has a render and update loop. The big technical challenge here was being able to set a virtual resolution and palette, along with fullscreen capability. Making the canvas fill the screen kept leading to smeared pixels from smooth scaling. The solution I found was to first render to a framebuffer, then present that framebuffer to the canvas. The palette and image data are merged together in a fragment shader.
